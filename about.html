Welcome to Regalia Event Management Company


Regalia Event Management is an ISO 9001:2015-certified event management company based in Kerala. With over 18 years of experience, we have grown from a local startup in Thrissur (established in 2008) into a premier statewide service provider. Specializing in wedding event management, corporate galas, and private celebrations, we have successfully managed over 2,500 events and served 3,500 satisfied clients. Whether it is a traditional Malayalee wedding or a luxury destination event for clients from abroad, we bring expertise and passion to every celebration in "God’s Own Country." Today, Regalia Event Management (informally known as Regalia Events) operates with a strong presence in Kochi, Thrissur, Calicut, and Trivandrum, supported by our own in-house production facilities to ensure a seamless, stress-free experience.


Our Core Services Wedding Management: Traditional, modern, and destination weddings. Corporate Events: Award nights, conferences, and team-building retreats. Inaugurations: Professional launches for retail and business ventures. Private Functions: Birthdays, Holy Communions, and regional celebrations. Venue Selection: Multi-locality assistance across the entire state of Kerala.
